Extremely fun facts about Los Angeles:

  • The iconic Hollywood Sign originally read "Hollywoodland" when it was erected in 1923 to advertise a real estate development. The "land" was dropped in 1949.
  • Griffith Park in Los Angeles is one of the largest urban parks in North America, spanning over 4,300 acres and home to the famous Griffith Observatory.
  • Los Angeles has the largest number of murals of any city in the world, showcasing its vibrant art culture and diverse community.

Facts about Los Angeles:

  1. The city's population: 3,979,576
  2. The year the city was incorporated: 1850
  3. The region of the state in which the city is located: Southern California
  4. The classification of the city: Urban
  5. The average income level of the city compared to the rest of the state: Higher
