Extremely fun facts about San Diego:

  • San Diego boasts nearly perfect weather year-round, with an average daily temperature of 70 degrees Fahrenheit and over 260 sunny days per year.
  • The Hotel Del Coronado, built in 1888, is the largest wooden structure in the United States and is a National Historic Landmark.
  • San Diego is home to the world-famous Comic-Con International convention.

Facts about San Diego:

  1. The city's population: 1,386,932
  2. The year the city was incorporated: 1850
  3. The region of the state in which the city is located: Southern California
  4. The classification of the city: Urban
  5. The average income level of the city compared to the rest of the state: Higher
